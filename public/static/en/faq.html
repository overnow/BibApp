Frequently Asked Questions

Where do the citations come from?
BibApp is populated directly by the citations that exist in the HSD Faculty Information System.

How do I add or change information in my profile?
Profiles are maintained by librarians at the Health Sciences Library. Please email DiscoverHSD@luc.edu with any changes or updates.

What kind of platform is Discover @ Loyola using?
Discover @ Loyola runs on BibApp, and open source software package that uses Ruby on Rails, Solr, and MySQL and is generously hosted by the HSD IT Department.
