About Discover @ Loyola

Discover @ Loyola uses linked data to highlight, expose, and record the scholarly output of the Health Sciences Division at Loyola University Chicago. In collaboration with the IT Department, the Health Sciences Library created this database with the following goals:

  • Serve as a repository of scholarly publications produced by the Health Sciences Division.
  • Help the global research community to locate experts in the field so as to facilitate scientific knowledge.
  • Assist researchers across campus in identifying local research experts for collaboration.

The pilot version of Discover @ Loyola features the scholarly output of the Oncology Institute, but will expand to include the Stritch School of Medicine, Marcella Niehoff School of Nursing, and the Graduate School with programs like the Institute of Public Health, the Neiswanger Institute for Bioethics and Health Policy, and the Leischner Institute for Medical Education.

If you'd like to be added to Discover, please contact your department liason.
